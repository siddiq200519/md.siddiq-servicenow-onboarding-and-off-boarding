# Strategic Business Objectives

**Project:** Automated Employee Onboarding & Offboarding System  
**Phase:** Requirement Analysis & Planning

---

## Objective 1: Streamline Employee Lifecycle Management

The primary goal involves digital transformation of onboarding and offboarding processes via ServiceNow. This initiative aims to eliminate manual intervention, standardize procedures, and enhance the overall employee journey experience.

**Core Goals:**
- Establish comprehensive workflow automation for employee onboarding and offboarding
- Minimize HR and administrative manual intervention
- Enforce standardized processes across all employee lifecycle stages
- Enhance the employee onboarding and offboarding journey

**Anticipated Results:**
- Accelerated onboarding timelines
- Minimized operational bottlenecks
- Optimized employee lifecycle administration

---

## Objective 2: Enhance HR and Manager Efficiency

HR personnel and managers require tools that empower self-service capabilities and automate approval chains to accelerate request handling and streamline departmental task distribution.

**Core Goals:**
- Deploy self-service catalog functionality for HR operations
- Establish automated approval chains and routing
- Configure intelligent task assignment mechanisms across departments
- Enhance visibility into lifecycle status for HR leadership

**Anticipated Results:**
- Shortened approval cycles
- Minimized cross-department coordination efforts
- Elevated HR team and manager productivity levels

---

## Objective 3: Ensure Secure Role-Based Access

Security measures must be implemented through role-based access control mechanisms to protect confidential employee data and restrict system functionality based on user responsibilities.

**Core Goals:**
- Establish Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) framework
- Configure Access Control Lists to prevent unauthorized data access
- Limit department visibility to their respective task assignments
- Safeguard sensitive employee information

**Anticipated Results:**
- Enhanced employee data protection
- Restricted access for HR, IT, Facilities, and Security personnel based on role
- Adherence to organizational security standards

---

## Objective 4: Enable Data-Driven HR Decisions

Implementing analytics and reporting infrastructure allows HR leadership to make informed decisions based on process metrics and performance indicators.

**Core Goals:**
- Establish monitoring for onboarding timelines and milestones
- Generate metrics on task completion performance
- Measure and track SLA compliance rates
- Identify and highlight process improvement opportunities

**Anticipated Results:**

* Improved visibility into HR processes
* Better decision-making using analytics
* Continuous improvement of employee lifecycle workflows

---

## 5. Support Organizational Compliance & Sustainability

**Description**
Maintain proper audit trails and shift from manual paperwork to digital processes.

**Key Objectives**

* Maintain audit trails for approvals and actions
* Automate access revocation during offboarding
* Digitize HR documentation and workflows
* Reduce dependency on paper-based processes

**Expected Outcome**

* Improved regulatory compliance
* Secure and structured offboarding procedures
* Environment-friendly digital workflows
