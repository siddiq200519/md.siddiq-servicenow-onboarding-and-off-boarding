# Functional Scope & Objectives

**Project:** Automated Employee Onboarding & Offboarding System  
**Phase:** Requirement Analysis & Planning

---

## 1. Streamline Employee Lifecycle Management

**Description**
Digitize and automate onboarding and offboarding processes through ServiceNow to reduce manual work, ensure consistency, and improve employee experience.

**Key Objectives**

* Automate employee onboarding and offboarding workflows
* Reduce manual HR and administrative work
* Maintain consistency in employee lifecycle processes
* Improve the overall employee onboarding experience

**Expected Outcome**

* Faster onboarding process
* Reduced operational delays
* Efficient employee lifecycle management

---

## 2. Enhance HR and Manager Efficiency

**Description**
Provide HR teams and managers with self-service catalog items and automated workflows to speed up approvals and task assignments.

**Key Objectives**

* Implement self-service service catalog items
* Automate approval workflows
* Enable automated task assignments to departments
* Improve lifecycle tracking for HR and management

**Expected Outcome**

* Faster approval processes
* Reduced manual coordination between departments
* Increased HR and managerial productivity

---

## 3. Ensure Secure Role-Based Access

**Description**
Implement role-based Access Control Lists (ACLs) to ensure secure access to employee information and system functionality.

**Key Objectives**

* Implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
* Configure ACLs to restrict unauthorized access
* Allow departments to access only relevant tasks
* Protect confidential employee data

**Expected Outcome**

* Secure employee data management
* Controlled access for HR, IT, Facilities, and Security teams
* Compliance with organizational security policies

---

## 4. Enable Data-Driven HR Decisions

**Description**
Use dashboards and reporting tools to analyze HR processes and improve operational efficiency.

**Key Objectives**

* Monitor onboarding timelines
* Track task completion rates
* Measure SLA adherence
* Identify workflow bottlenecks

**Expected Outcome**

* Improved visibility into HR processes
* Better decision-making using analytics
* Continuous improvement of employee lifecycle workflows

---

## 5. Support Organizational Compliance & Sustainability

**Description**
Maintain proper audit trails and shift from manual paperwork to digital processes.

**Key Objectives**

* Maintain audit trails for approvals and actions
* Automate access revocation during offboarding
* Digitize HR documentation and workflows
* Reduce dependency on paper-based processes

**Expected Outcome**

* Improved regulatory compliance
* Secure and structured offboarding procedures
* Environment-friendly digital workflows

---
