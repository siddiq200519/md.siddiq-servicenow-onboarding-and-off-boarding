# Stakeholder Roles & Responsibilities

**Project:** Automated Employee Onboarding & Offboarding System  
**Phase:** Requirement Analysis & Planning

---

## Overview

The solution leverages collaboration among HR personnel, management staff, IT specialists, facilities coordinators, and security personnel through the ServiceNow platform. Each team manages domain-specific activities aligned with their expertise. Role-based security mechanisms restrict visibility and actions to tasks within each team's scope.

---

## Organizational Stakeholder Map

### Human Resources (HR) Team

**Primary Function:** Request initiation and employee record management

**Key Activities:**
- Originate onboarding and offboarding request submissions
- Preserve employee documentation and compliance records
- Process requests via ServiceNow interface
- Validate accuracy of submitted employee information
- Facilitate inter-departmental communication during lifecycle stages

---

### Management Personnel

**Primary Function:** Request authorization and lifecycle oversight

**Key Activities:**
- Authorize or decline onboarding request submissions
- Approve employee separation and offboarding procedures
- Ensure departmental task completion and compliance

---

### Information Technology Department

**Primary Function:** System access provisioning and account management

**Key Activities:**
- Establish employee user accounts and access credentials
- Configure system access for required business applications
- Terminate digital system access upon employee separation

---

### Facilities & Space Management

**Primary Function:** Physical resource allocation and asset management

**Key Activities:**
- Assign workspace and workstation assignments
- Distribute workplace equipment and office supplies
- Recover organizational assets during exit processes

---

### Security & Compliance Team

**Primary Function:** Physical access control and policy enforcement

**Key Activities:**
- Authorize facility premises access
- Administer security protocols and standards
- Revoke physical building access upon employee departure

---

## Intended Results

- Transparent role delineation across all departments
- Streamlined cross-functional collaboration
- Secure and well-structured employee lifecycle procedures
